🏆 The Playbook

All 7 Challenges

CHALLENGE 1
😶 Blindfold Relay + Fire Puzzle
🏃 Physical 🧩 Puzzle 🤝 Team
🏠 Backyard⏱ ~20 min🕑 2:00 PM
Setup

9 drawstring bags (3 per tribe, color-coded) with Fire Puzzle pieces. Hidden on fence posts, tree branches, railings. 1 caller per tribe (best for older guests). 5 others blindfolded.

Rules

Callers direct blindfolded teammates by voice only. Find all 3 bags, remove blindfolds, assemble Vexzle Fire Puzzle. First tribe finished wins.

Supplies
  • 3 Vexzle Fire Puzzles (Party Pack #4)
  • 15+ blindfolds
  • 9 drawstring bags
Scoring: 1st = 3pts, 2nd = 2pts, 3rd = 1pt
"For today's first challenge, you will be blindfolded. One person from your tribe will be your eyes. They will guide you through the yard to find your puzzle pieces. Once you have all the pieces, rip off those blindfolds and solve the Fire Puzzle. First tribe to finish wins reward. Survivors ready? GO!"
CHALLENGE 2
🧠 Jessica / 5th Grade Trivia
🧠 Mental 🤝 Team
🏠 Home⏱ ~15 min🕑 2:30 PM
Setup

3 whiteboards + markers. 15 to 20 question cards: half "How Well Do You Know Jessica?" and half 5th grade curriculum.

Sample Questions
  • "What position did Jessica play at Sonoma State?"
  • "What is 7 x 8?"
  • "What is the capital of California?"
  • "Spell NECESSARY"
Rules

Jeff reads question. 30 seconds to discuss. Reveal simultaneously. 1 point per correct answer.

Supplies
  • 3 whiteboards + markers
  • Printed question cards (DIY)
Scoring: 1st = 3pts, 2nd = 2pts, 3rd = 1pt
⚠ ACTION: Collect 10 to 15 personal questions from Stephanie at least 2 weeks before.
"For four years, Jessica shaped young minds at Parkmead Elementary. Today, we find out if her family is smarter than her 5th graders. Tribes, grab your whiteboards. This is a test of knowledge, memory, and how well you really know the birthday girl."
CHALLENGE 3
⚽ Soccer Shootout
🏃 Physical 🤝 Team
🏞 Diablo Vista Park⏱ ~15 min🕑 3:00 PM
Setup

Mini obstacle course: 6 cones to weave, tarp to crawl under, pop-up goal. 15ft adults, 10ft kids. Older guests skip obstacle. Helper

Rules

Each member runs course, takes penalty kick. Goal = 1 point. Most goals wins.

Supplies
  • 12 cones
  • 1 to 2 pop-up goals
  • 2 to 3 soccer balls
  • 1 tarp
Scoring: 1st = 3pts, 2nd = 2pts, 3rd = 1pt
"Thirty years ago, a soccer star was born in Antioch. She dominated the fields at Deer Valley High. She played at Sonoma State. Today, we find out if the people who love her can even score a single goal. No pressure."
CHALLENGE 4
🥶 Frozen T-Shirt (SURPRISE)
🏃 Physical 😂 Comedy 🤝 Team
🏠 Backyard⏱ ~5 to 10 min🕑 4:30 PM
Setup (April 29)

3 days before: Soak 3 T-shirts, fold into rectangles, parchment paper between each, freeze. Pull into cooler morning of.

Rules

SURPRISE reveal. Each tribe unfolds a frozen shirt and puts it on one teammate. No tools. Hands, body heat, smashing on ground allowed.

Supplies
  • 3 plain T-shirts
  • Parchment paper
  • Freezer space
Scoring: 1st = 3pts, 2nd = 2pts, 3rd = 1pt
"For this next challenge, I need each tribe to send someone to grab what's behind me. [Reveal frozen shirts] These shirts have been frozen solid for three days. Your tribe must unfold it and put it on one of your members. No tools. No hot water. Just your bare hands. Survivors ready? GO!"
CHALLENGE 5
🔡 Word Scramble Relay
🏃 Physical 🧠 Mental 🤝 Team
🏠 Backyard⏱ ~15 min🕑 4:45 PM
Setup

3 stations ~30 feet from start. Scrambled letter tiles. Each member runs, unscrambles one word, tags next. Final person assembles: HAPPY 30TH JESSICA.

Word List
  • RCOESC = SOCCER
  • HCEETRA = TEACHER
  • VSIUORRV = SURVIVOR
  • NTOHAIC = ANTIOCH
  • MAOOSN = SONOMA
  • BIADOL = DIABLO
Supplies
  • Cardstock letter tiles (DIY)
  • 3 tables
  • Tape for wind
Scoring: 1st = 3pts, 2nd = 2pts, 3rd = 1pt
"This challenge will test your body AND your brain. One at a time, sprint to your station, unscramble the word, and race back. But here's the twist: your last player must take all the words and unscramble the final phrase. The answer? It's something very special. Survivors ready? GO!"
CHALLENGE 6
🎨 Touchy Subjects with Balloons
🗣 Social 👑 Individual
🏠 Home⏱ ~15 min🕑 5:15 PM
Setup

18 foam boards with 2 balloons each + player name. Helper inflates 36 balloons morning of (20 to 30 min).

Rules

Jeff reads prompt. Everyone writes who they think the majority picks. Correct = pop someone's balloon. 2 pops = out. Last standing wins.

Sample Prompts
  • "Who is most likely to win a real season of Survivor?"
  • "Who would be voted out first?"
  • "Who is the biggest drama queen?"
  • "Who would you trust to babysit your kids?"
  • "Who is secretly the most competitive?"
  • "Most likely to cry at Final Tribal?"
  • "Who would betray their alliance first?"
  • "Who is the funniest person here?"
Supplies
  • 36 balloons
  • 18 foam boards
  • Thumbtack for popping
  • Dry erase boards (reuse from Trivia)
Scoring: Individual winner = 5pts, runner-up = 3pts
"This challenge is called Touchy Subjects. I'm going to ask you what you REALLY think about each other. If you guess what the majority says, you earn the right to pop someone's balloon. Two pops and you're out. Last one standing wins. Let's see how well you know this group... and how ruthless you're willing to be."
CHALLENGE 7
🧩 Final Immunity: Tree Puzzle
🧩 Puzzle 🤝 Team Reps
🏠 Backyard / Garage⏱ ~15 min🕑 7:00 PM
Setup

3 Vexzle Tree Puzzles on separate tables. Pieces loose in bags.

Rules

Each tribe picks 2 representatives. All 3 pairs race simultaneously. First pair to complete wins Final Immunity.

Supplies
  • 3 Vexzle Tree Puzzles (Party Pack #3)
  • 3 tables
Scoring: Winning tribe = 5pts per member
"This is it. The final immunity challenge. Each tribe will select two people to represent them. Three puzzles. Three pairs. One winner. The pair that finishes first guarantees their tribe a spot in Survivor history. Choose wisely. This is for IMMUNITY."

🍺 The Hidden Immunity Ice

Runs all evening. This is the game within the game.

  • Supply: 24 Smirnoff Ices (two 12-packs)
  • Wave 1 (8:30 PM): Plant 6 bottles in coolers, under chairs, bathroom
  • Wave 2 (9:30 PM): Plant 6 more in sneakier spots: inside Tree Mail scrolls, taped under chairs, inside voting urn
  • Wave 3 (10:00 PM+): Remaining 12 in increasingly obvious places
  • Rule: If you see it, touch it, or are handed it, drop to one knee and chug. "The tribe has spoken." Cannot refuse.
  • Emergency Out: "Play your idol" and assign it to someone else.

🎯 Game Rules

🍺 Tribal Spirits Council

12 Survivor-themed "Most Likely To" prompts. Everyone points at their answer on the count of 3. Majority rules. Person voted gets a drink.

  • Most likely to win a real season of Survivor
  • Most likely to betray their alliance on Day 1
  • Most likely to cry at Tribal Council
  • Most likely to find a Hidden Immunity Idol
  • Most likely to eat a bug for the win
  • Most likely to get blindsided
  • Most likely to give an iconic confessional
  • Most likely to flirt their way to the final 3
  • Most likely to quit the game
  • Most likely to be brought back for a returning players season
  • Most likely to make the jury bitter
  • Most likely to become the next Jeff Probst

🧵 Buff Chug Relay

Re-form original tribes (The Yoc, Menalua, Diabla). Each tribe lines up relay style. Each member must chug a drink, then wrap their buff around the next person's head before they can go. Penalty: if the buff falls off at any point, the person must restart their chug. First tribe to finish wins.

🔥 Fire Making Challenge (Flip Cup Tournament)

Single elimination bracket. Draw the bracket on a whiteboard. Play tribal council ambience music. Each match is a best of 3 flip cup round. Loser gets their torch "snuffed" (phone flashlight turned off dramatically). Champion wins the title of Fire Making King/Queen.

💰 Survivor Auction: Happy Hour Edition

Use leftover Survivor Bucks. 6 quick items:

  • Mystery shot (could be good, could be terrible)
  • Premium mixed drink for buyer + friend
  • Steal someone else's drink (advantage item)
  • Immunity from the next Ice (lasts 30 min)
  • Choose someone who must take a shot
  • Everyone drinks (group item, revealed)

🌟 Bonus Games

📜 "The Jury Speaks"

Anonymous roast cards of Jessica. Hand out index cards earlier in the evening. Everyone writes one roast or funny memory. Charlie reads them aloud in Jeff Probst voice at the podium. Jessica guesses who wrote each one. Correct guess = writer drinks. Wrong guess = Jessica drinks.

🏝 "Exile Island"

Consensus vote exiles someone to an uncomfortable chair (lawn chair, stool, etc.) for 5 minutes. The exiled player gets a consolation drink and a clue to the location of a hidden Smirnoff Ice. They must serve their time before returning to the group.

Outteach. Outplay. Outthirty.
